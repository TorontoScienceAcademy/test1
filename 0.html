TSA , is an institution of higher learning that promotes and inculcates ethical standards, values and norms. The Central mission of TSA is to provide students with opportunities, resources and expertise to achieve academic, personal and career goals within a stimulating and supportive environment. In addition to creation and dissemination of knowledge, its mission includes promotion of humanism and peace through higher education . TSA provides an inspiring environment for students seeking a rich learning environment that goes beyond the ordinary school curriculum. Sensitive to the needs of its students and staff, TSA is committed to providing a humane, responsive and invigorating atmosphere for productive learning and innovative thinking.
